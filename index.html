Diplomatic Corps Field Assignments

Classified birthday operations — top-secret fun only

# Codename Objective
1 Operation Moonshot Acquire three discreet photographs of “the ambassador’s full moon.” Evidence for internal security files only.
2 Treaty of Matrimony Stage a public proposal to a delegate chosen by the committee, complete with ring-shaped object and speech.
3 Rapid-Fire Diplomacy Down three shots, hands-free, within 20 seconds — proof you thrive under pressure.
4 Uniform Protocol Upgrade Exchange current undergarments for a diplomatic-issue thong (or procure one). Document the wardrobe change.
5 Silent Summit Hold 90 seconds of eye contact with the most provocative envoy present. No laughter allowed.
6 Humour Exchange Agreement Tell a joke to a random civilian. If they laugh, treaty ratified; if not, perform a three-step hop as reparations.
7 Bilateral Selfie Accords Secure an individual selfie with every delegation member; each image must show a peace sign.
8 Mayonnaise Protocol Decommission an entire pot of mayo using only a spoon. Indigestion tablets will be provided.
9 Compliment Clock Convention On every half-hour chime, deliver a sincere compliment to everyone present. Punctuality required.

Complete all nine missions to earn full diplomatic immunity — and eternal birthday glory. Godspeed, envoy!
